I sketch for coffee!

There's no feeling quite like pencil against real paper! Sketching traditionally was how I learned to draw and is something I still indulge in from time to time. I work mainly with ink and pencils on marker paper, and love doing character portraits!

Over on my Ko-Fi page I'm offering bust sketches in pencil for just 3 coffees ($9). And for only ($12) I can send your sketch to you in the mail! These sketches are cleanly drawn and shaded in pencil on smooth 5x7 marker grade paper. They frame well and are perfect for hanging above your desk! Toss me a few coffees and get a sketch of your favorite waifu to hang above your desk, so that during those tough moments at work, you can remember to do it for her!
