Commission Info

Thinking about getting me to draw for you? Well, you've come to the right place! On this page you'll find my price sheet with different pricing tiers along with some examples of each.

Depending on demand, commissions take anywhere from 1-2 weeks for me to finish. I try to remain in close contact with my commissioners via email/twitter/discord, and will let you know if there are any delays. To get your commission started, please fill out the google form below or if you need extra help, navigate to the contact page and message me your info. I'll contact you back via email and confirm the details. Once ready, I'll send you a link to pay with paypal, venmo, or crypto (if you're into that sort of thing). After I get started on your piece I'll send you a sketch update and give 1 opportunity for minor edits.

For the most part I do take NSFW commissions! But make sure to message me and ask if you're worried about whether or not I'll accept your commission.
